OSP Desk
  • Name: OSP Desk
  • Designer(s): Nonpareil
  • Builder(s): Mathieu Gabiot
  • Category: furniture
  • Tags: desk, Open Source Publishing, funiture
  • Created: June 2016
  • Location: Brussels
  • Required hardware: panel saw, biscuit joiner, biscuits, clamps
  • License: ï»¿CERN Open Hardware Licence v1.2
  • Client: OSP asbl

A simple desk that can be easily mount and unmount, two comfortable places with the ability to work face to face or on the side. Some little storage, close to hand for daily stuff. A mobile printer module for A3 printer with storage for two sizes of sheets.
